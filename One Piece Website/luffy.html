Biography

Monkey D. Luffy, also known as "Straw Hat Luffy" serves as the main protagonist in the story of One Piece. He is the captain of the "Straw Hat Pirates" an infamous group of notorious pirates that sail across the Grand Line searching for the One Piece. Luffy was born and raised in "Foosha Village" a small village located in the East Blue along side his brother Portgas D. Ace. At the age of 7 Luffy's body turned into rubber reason being that he accidentally ate the devil fruit, "Gum-Gum". During his childhood he befriended a pirate known as "Red-Haired Shanks" who would have a larger influence on Luffy's life. Shanks gave Luffy his iconic Straw Hat signifying Luffy's promise that they would meet again one fateful day as the most notorious pirate that ever sailed the seas.

Arsenal and Fun facts

  • Luffy's primary abilities consist of increased strength, speed, durability and endurance.
  • Since Luffy posesses a devil fruit he is not able to swim.
  • Luffy seemingly does not gain weight.
