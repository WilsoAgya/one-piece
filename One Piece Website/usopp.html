Overview

Usopp is the sniper for the Straw Hat Pirates and is the third member to join the Straw Hats. He was born in a village known as Syrup village and founded his own group of pirates known as the "Usopp Pirates" He is known to be a compulsive liar and a trickster throughout his village creating traps and other devices for the sake of joking around. Usopp's motives for being a pirate is so that he can become a brave warrior of the sea just like his father.

Arsenal and Fun facts

  • Usopp's weapon of choice is a sling shot
  • Usopp is based on the Japanese mythical species tengu, who are said to have long noses and have habits of trickinig people.
