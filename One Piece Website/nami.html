Biography

Nami also known as "Cat Burglar" Nami is the Straw Hat Pirate's navigator and the second member to join the Straw Hat Pirates. She was raised in a village known as Cocoyasi Village along side her adoptive sister Nojiko. Nami and Nojiko were taken in by a lady named "Bell-Mere" and the trio over the years would be become very close up until Bell-Mere's early death. Nami originally joined the Straw Hats so that she could rob them and buy back her village from Arlong, but after the Straw Hats defeated Arlong she became a legitimate member of the Straw Hat Pirates. Nami's reason for being a pirate is so that she can achieve her goal creating a map of the entire ocean.

Arsenal and Fun facts

  • Nami's weapon is The Clima-Tact, a device that is able to control the weather
  • Nami is the only Straw Hat to invite someone to join the Straw Hat Pirates before Luffy could ask them as she invited Chopper before Luffy did.
  • Nami's favourite food are tangerine oranges
